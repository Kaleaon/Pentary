What is Balanced Pentary?

Technology

Instead of just 0 and 1, we use 5 states: -2, -1, 0, +1, +2.

Binary (Base-2)

0110 1010
0000 0000 (Zero is costly)
1111 0001 (Negative is complex)

Balanced Pentary (Base-5)

1 2 0 -1
0 (Zero is natural state)
-1 -2 1 (Negative is native)

Key Advantage: Neural Networks use positive and negative weights constantly. Binary chips need awkward "Sign bits" and "Two's Complement" math. Pentary chips do it natively.
