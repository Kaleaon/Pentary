## 3. Business Model

### 3.1 Revenue Streams

#### 1. IP Licensing (40% of revenue)
**Model**: License pentary IP to chip manufacturers
- **License Fee**: $5M-$10M per customer
- **Royalty**: 3-5% of chip sales
- **Target**: 10 licensees by 2030
- **Revenue**: $1.5B by 2030

**Customers**:
- Smartphone manufacturers (Samsung, Xiaomi)
- Automotive (Tesla, Waymo)
- IoT companies (Amazon, Google)

#### 2. Custom Chips (40% of revenue)
**Model**: Design and sell custom pentary chips
- **Price**: $50-$200 per chip
- **Volume**: 20M chips/year by 2030
- **Revenue**: $1.5B by 2030

**Products**:
- Edge AI accelerator ($50)
- Data center inference chip ($200)
- Automotive AI chip ($100)

#### 3. Software and Services (20% of revenue)
**Model**: Sell software tools and support
- **Compiler toolchain**: $10K/seat
- **Development kits**: $5K each
- **Support contracts**: $50K-$500K/year
- **Revenue**: $750M by 2030

**Services**:
- Model optimization
- Custom development
- Training and certification
- Technical support
