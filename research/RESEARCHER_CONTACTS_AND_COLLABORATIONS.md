# Pentary Computing: Potential Research Collaborators and Contacts

This document compiles researchers, labs, and organizations working on technologies relevant to Pentary computing who may be interested in collaboration.

---

## Executive Summary

Pentary computing bridges multiple research domains:
- **Multi-Valued Logic (MVL)**: Non-binary computation paradigms
- **Neural Network Quantization**: Low-bit weight representations
- **In-Memory Computing**: Memristors, crossbar arrays
- **Neuromorphic Computing**: Brain-inspired architectures
- **AI Accelerators**: Custom silicon for ML

The researchers below have published work directly applicable to Pentary architecture.
